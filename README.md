# Thanh-Nghia Truong

AI Researcher / Applied AI Engineer

Assistant Professor at the WACOM-TUAT Joint Research Lab, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology.

I work on AI systems for handwritten and visual information, especially handwritten mathematical expression recognition, document analysis, digital ink processing, graph neural networks, multimodal learning, and automatic scoring of handwritten answers.
