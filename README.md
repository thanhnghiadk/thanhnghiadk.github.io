# Thanh-Nghia Truong

Assistant Professor / Applied AI Researcher / Data Scientist

Assistant Professor at the WACOM-TUAT Joint Research Lab, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology.

I work on AI systems for handwritten and visual information, especially handwritten mathematical expression recognition, document analysis, digital ink processing, graph neural networks, multimodal learning, automatic scoring of handwritten answers, and recommendation-oriented data-driven AI systems.
